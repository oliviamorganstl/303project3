This website is sponsored by the Bureau of Criminal Investigation. While many websites already exist to serve the purpose of educating Americans, this Utah department wants to make sure this website stays relevant to serving the needs of the community.


This new page will serve to have updated blog posts answering questions or informing our readers about new information. Please use the "Contact" section of our website to submit a form with any blog posts you would like to read. Keep checking back to see when a blog post with your specific need will be posted. And in the meantime, stay safe :)
